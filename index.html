   Exchangerzone, Inc. is the Afghanistan first and largest private financial company founded by Investone Corps, Inc. (ICI) in August 2015. Exchangerzone was registered with Afghanistan Investment Support Agency (AISA) under license number D-80624 as a private corporation in 2016. It was also duly registered with Da Afghanistan Bank (DAB) under license number 423 and license number 201607 to offer financial services. Exchangerzone is a fully-fledged financial company, which offers wide range of quality financial services within Afghanistan and abroad. The company specializes in foreign exchange, money transfer, microfinance, investment schemes, financial consulting and other selected financial services. We are the first private company in Afghanistan outside the banking industry to offer specialized foreign exchange, remittance, microfinance, investment, financial consulting and other selected financial services
